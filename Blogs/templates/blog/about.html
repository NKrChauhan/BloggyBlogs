ABOUT US




Nitish is a director of brand marketing, with experience managing global teams and multi-million-dollar campaigns. Her background in brand strategy, visual design, and account management inform her mindful but competitive approach. Nitish is fueled by her passion for understanding the nuances of cross-cultural advertising. Meanwhile, she vastly improved the productivity of her team by implementing strategic project management strategies and ensuring a work-life balance for her department Nitish believes mindfulness in the workplace is key to success - a tenet she lives out through her interests in yoga, meditation, gardening, and painting.
